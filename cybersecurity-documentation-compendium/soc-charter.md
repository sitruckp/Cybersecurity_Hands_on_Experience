# Security Operations Center (SOC) Charter

**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)  
**Prepared by:** Kevin P. Curtis  
**Date:** April 2026  
**Classification:** Confidential – Internal Use Only

## 1. Purpose and Mission
Formal establishment of the SOC to strengthen cybersecurity through proactive monitoring, incident response, and collaborative risk management in a higher-education environment.

## 2. Objectives
- Foundational monitoring and detection
- Timely triage and response
- Blue Team first, then controlled Purple Team activities
- Institutional knowledge retention

## 3. Scope
**In Scope (Initial Phases):** Monitoring, triage, authorized testing under ROE, Blue Team development.  
**Out of Scope (Initial Phases):** 24×7 staffing, physical security, legal/HR actions.

## 4. Authority and Responsibilities
SOC authorized to monitor and recommend; execution by Server Operations. Full authority details included.

## 5. Organizational Structure and Roles
- SOC Lead/Coordinator
- Blue Team Analysts
- Red/Purple Team functions (post-maturity)
- Liaisons with other teams

## 6. Governance, Metrics, and Oversight
Metrics: MTTD, MTTE, alert fidelity, coverage progress. Non-punitive.

## 7. Implementation and Transition Approach
Builds on 90-day and 180-day plans, ROE, policies, and hybrid transition from outsourced MDR.

## 8. Data Privacy and Academic Considerations
Minimum necessary access, respect for FERPA, research confidentiality, and academic freedom.

## 9. Review and Approval
Annual review. Signatures for Vice President and Server Operations Director.
